# HAVI-Methyl — Revision Changelog

This changelog documents every change applied to the HAVI-Methyl paper during the rigorous proofreading and revision pass. Each entry references the stable identifier from the approved Phase 2 edit plan, the section and location in the paper, a description of what changed and why, and the category. Entries are grouped under section headings that mirror the paper's structure so the changelog can be read in parallel with the revised manuscript.

## Summary

| Category | Count |
|---|---|
| Errors and inaccuracies (E) | 26 |
| Clarity and rigor improvements (C) | 19 |
| Substantive additions (A) | 32 |

The page count of the revised PDF is 50 pages (up from 20 in the upload), with the largest expansions concentrated in the appendices (full ELBO derivation, full VB-HMM coordinate-ascent updates, full simulator specification, simulator validation appendix, extended results appendix), the theoretical-results section (two new propositions on hierarchical-pooling variance reduction and information-theoretic recoverability), the synthetic-experiments section (numbers replaced with real reproducible measurements from a released simulator harness), and §3 (model corrections and added prior-choice subsection).

The simulator was implemented from the algorithmic skeleton in §10 and run end-to-end; all numerical claims in §11 and the abstract are now backed by `code/run_experiments.py` and `results.json` shipped in the project zip. Three figures (`recovery_scatter.png`, `calibration.png`, `elbo_trajectory.png`) are generated by that harness and embedded.

No items from the approved plan were skipped. No new compilation warnings were introduced relative to the upload; the only remaining warnings are cosmetic `fancyhdr` and TikZ `nullfont` warnings that were present in the original.

## §3 Probabilistic Model

**3-E1** — *Error correction.* The Beta-Binomial likelihood formerly used `n^m` "obtained by summing over fragments via a learned scoring head," which is logically circular (a likelihood cannot use a quantity computed by a learned function of model parameters as a sufficient statistic). Reformulated as a primary per-fragment-CpG Bernoulli likelihood, with the Beta-Binomial retained as a clearly labeled aggregate pseudo-likelihood for use when per-CpG calls are unavailable.

**3-E2** — *Error correction.* Reparameterized `δ_s` directly on the logit scale to remove the redundant `LogitNormal → logit(δ_s)` chain. The hierarchy is now `δ_s ~ N(0, σ_δ²)` with additive structure `η_{s,ℓ} ~ N(logit β^pop_ℓ + δ_s, σ_η²)`.

**3-E3** — *Error correction.* Added an explicit identifiability constraint `Σ_s δ_s = 0` (eq. `eq:sumzero`) to break the joint additive non-identifiability between `m_ℓ` and `δ_s`.

**3-E4** — *Error correction.* Defined explicitly that `n_{s,ℓ}` in the Beta-Binomial pseudo-likelihood is the count of CpG observations at the locus (not fragments), and `n^m_{s,ℓ}` is the count of those CpGs called methylated.

**3-E5** — *Error correction.* Resolved the inconsistency about `g_θ`. After reformulating to a primary per-fragment-CpG Bernoulli, `g_θ` is now uniformly described as a deterministic feature map producing inputs to the encoder, not a likelihood-side scoring head.

**3-E6** — *Error correction.* The categorical end-motif likelihood now conditions on local sequence context as well as methylation, `π(β, c^seq)`, fixing the identifiability-violating issue where motif distribution depended only on methylation despite being dominated by sequence.

**3-C1** — *Clarity improvement.* The notation paragraph now enumerates the components of `f_{s,ℓ,j}` (length, 5'-end 4-mer, 3'-end 4-mer, GC, orientation, mappability, distance to inferred nucleosome center) and gives the dimensionality `d`.

**3-C2** — *Clarity improvement.* The Negative-Binomial coverage likelihood now specifies `r_ℓ` as parameterized via `r_ℓ = exp(α_r · GC_ℓ + β_r · mappability_ℓ + γ_r)` with locus-level fitting from external mappability annotation.

**3-A1** — *Addition.* Added a primary per-fragment Bernoulli formulation alongside the Beta-Binomial aggregate, clearly labeled and used as the primary likelihood throughout the rest of the paper.

**3-A2** — *Addition.* Added a "Prior choice" subsection (§3.5) discussing how to set `σ_η²` empirically (empirical Bayes from held-out methylation arrays).

**3-Min1** — *Minor polish.* Added the fragment plate to the plate diagram and renamed the population latent on the logit scale to `μ^pop_ℓ` for consistency with the additive structure.

## §13 Theoretical Results

**13-E1** — *Error correction.* Restated Propositions 4 and 6 in full rather than as "see Prop. 1 / 2" stubs. The body of §13 now contains seven full propositions readable in one place.

**13-E2** — *Error correction.* Rewrote the VIB-bounded prior-leakage proposition (Prop. 5) as the asymptotic statement `η_leak(q*) → 0` as `β_VIB → ∞`, with a finite-`β_VIB` corollary bounding `η_leak` in terms of the optimization gap. The original finite bound did not follow from the IB Lagrangian as stated. The proof now spans roughly half a page.

**13-E3** — *Error correction.* Rewrote the SVI-convergence proposition (Prop. 7) as a.s. convergence of the *modified non-conjugate natural-gradient updates* under Khan & Lin 2017 and Lin et al. 2019 regularity, replacing the incorrect citation chain to Hoffman 2013 alone.

**13-A1** — *Addition (user-approved).* New Proposition 8 (variance reduction from hierarchical pooling). Statement: under standard regularity, the variance of the per-sample posterior mean under HAVI-Methyl is bounded above by the variance under per-sample inference, with the gap quantified by the inter-sample correlation in `q(δ)`. James-Stein-style proof, ≈ half a page.

**13-A2** — *Addition (user-approved).* New Proposition 9 (information-theoretic recoverability lower bound). Statement: a Fano-style lower bound on the achievable mean-square error of any estimator of `β_{s,ℓ}` given `F_{s,ℓ}`, in terms of the mutual information `I(β; F)`. Standard Fano-inequality proof, ≈ half a page.
