\subsection*{Data flow}
For one $(s,\ell)$ pair, fragment features pass through ISAB layers and PMA pooling to form $c^{\mathrm{frag}}_{s,\ell}$. The sequence window around $\ell$ is encoded into $c^{\mathrm{seq}}_\ell$. Current variational means and local coverage are concatenated with these embeddings and passed through a context MLP. A flow sample produces $\eta_{s,\ell}$, likelihood heads evaluate available observation terms, and optional auxiliary heads evaluate ToO, VIB, mQTL, and domain losses.

\subsection*{Memory footprint}
At $|B_s|=8$, $|B_\ell|=4096$, mean fragments $\bar n=20$, hidden dimension $128$, and two ISAB layers, raw activation storage for a single tensor of shape $|B_s||B_\ell|\bar n d_c$ is approximately $8\cdot4096\cdot20\cdot128\cdot4\approx 336$~MB in float32 before accounting for attention projections, residuals, gradients, optimizer buffers, and checkpointing. The previous sub-100~MB estimate was too low for full training. A production implementation should measure peak memory under the actual framework, precision, checkpointing scheme, and sequence-encoder caching strategy.
